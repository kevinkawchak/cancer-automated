The section-by-section analysis is the bridge between the recorded technical
evidence and the enactable text: every statutory section traces back to a finding
in Section~\ref{sec:findings} and forward to a source in the section-to-source map
maintained with this bundle. That map is referenced rather than repeated here, so
that a reader who wishes to audit any clause can move from the clause to its
finding and from the finding to the recorded artifact in a single, unbroken
chain.
