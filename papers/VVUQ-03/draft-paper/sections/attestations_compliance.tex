tree, a description of the training data sets, and a formal attestation that the
system complies with bias-reduction and evidence-based clinical criteria. Require
the attestation to certify, under the penalties the enforcement section specifies,
each of: that the VVUQ verification process cleared before code generation (the
verification-before-generation certification, the core of this bill); that each
gate met its threshold bound to a named external standard; that the site PSL gate
passed and the robot USL score met the minimum for the procedure type; that the
algorithm and its training data minimize the risk of bias on protected
characteristics and adhere to evidence-based clinical guidelines, consistent with
the New York certification (\texttt{ny-a9149}) and the federal nondiscrimination
guarantees (\texttt{usc-titlevi}, \texttt{usc-ada}); and that a licensed human
retained the medical-necessity decision, consistent with California
(\texttt{ca-sb1120}) and the Florida human-review requirement (\texttt{fl-hb527}).
Tie the records integrity to HIPAA and the electronic-records rule
(\texttt{cfr-hipaa}, \texttt{cfr-part11}) and to risk management
(\texttt{iso14971}).]
