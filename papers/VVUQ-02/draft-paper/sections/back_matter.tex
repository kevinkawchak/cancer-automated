\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Data Availability}
\section*{Data Availability}
\label{sec:data}

All code, configuration, execution records, and figure scripts supporting this
manuscript are openly available in the \texttt{kevinkawchak/cancer-automated}
repository under \texttt{papers/VVUQ-02/} (inputs, codegen, execution, imagegen,
and this draft-paper), and are archived on Zenodo. The raw level-0 humanoid sensor
stream is deposited to Zenodo and referenced by per-iteration pointer files rather
than committed. All datasets are synthetic or illustrative and reproducible from
the deterministic seed 20260525 described in the Methods; no patient-identifiable
information is included.
